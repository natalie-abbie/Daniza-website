11 Days True Wilderness Safari

The 11 days Safari trip will start and end in Kampala, Uganda or Entebbe. The Safari will take you to the most exciting destinations that will offer you an amazing experience with Nature culture, and wildlife adventures. These are Murchison Falls NP, Lake Mburo NP, Bwindi Impenetrable Forest for Gorilla trekking, Lake Bunyonyi, Queen Elizabeth and Ishasha sector and finally Kibale NP for Chempanzee trekking with the surrounding places and Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary.

  • Day 1

    Upon arrival at Entebbe Airport, you will be welcomed by our Tour Guide and head to 2Friends hotel in Entebbe for a day relaxation, Diner and overnight at 2 Friends hotel.

  • Day 2

    Transfer to Murchison falls National Park
    After your breakfast at 2 friends, Drive to Murchison falls National Park for Big Five Trial via Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for Rhino Tracking, After Rhino tacking you will have lunch alongside the way and afterwards continue your journey to Murchison Falls National Park, you will then proceed to visit the top of the falls and later drive to the lodge where you will sleep. Dinner and overnight at Paraa Safari lodge.

  • Day 3

    Morning game drive and boat cruise
    After breakfast do an early morning game drive, look out for animals like lions, elephants, jackals, giraffes, buffalos, hyenas, leopards, Birds among others before they return to their hiding places come back for relaxation and lunch. After go for exciting boat trip up stream to the bottom of the falls on the Victoria Nile which will reward with the clear and fantastic view of animals like Hippos, Elephants,Crocodiles as well as bird species. Dinner and overnight at Paraa Safari lodge.

  • Day 4

    Transfer to Kibale National park
    After breakfast, you will embark on a scenic Uganda highlights safari road trip southwards to Kibale via Fort portal city, a beautiful city overlooking the Mountains of the moon (SNOW CAPED mountain). Taking the western scenic route to Kibale National Park, this journey will take you through the Albertine rift found in the western Arm of the Great East African rift valley. You will reach Fort portal early evening proceed to your Lodge to check in Have Dinner and overnight stay at Chimpanzee Guest House.

  • Day 5

    Chimpanzee tracking and Afternoon transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park. A relatively early drive to headquarters for briefing and embark on memorable experience of chimpanzee trekking lead by an experienced guide who will call them by, 1 hour for observation and photography, along the way you can also stand a chance to see other primates such as red-tailed monkeys, black and white colobus monkeys among others. after lunch you will transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park crossing the equator, reach in the evening. Check in to your lodge. Dinner and overnight stay at Mweya safari lodge.

  • Day 6

    Morning Game drive and Afternoon Kazinga Channel Boat cruise. After a cup of coffee or full breakfast, you will head out for an early morning savanna game drive up to Kasenyi plains in the park. You will look out for lions. Leopards, Cape buffaloes, Uganda Kobs, spotted hyenas, Mangoose, waterbucks, elephants warthogs among others. You will then drive back to the lodge an early lunch break. In the afternoon, after your lunch proceed down to the Mweya Peninsular and take a Boat cruise on Kazinga channel here you stand chances to see lots of Hippopotamus, crocodiles, Buffaloes and Elephants coming to drink and many different bird species. Return to the lodge Dinner and overnight stay (DAY 5)

  • Day 7

    : Morning game drive – transfer to BINP Early morning game drive, lunch and drive through Ishasha sector view tree climbing lions and transfer to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. Dinner and overnight at Mahogany springs lodge, Buhoma

  • Day 8

    : Gorilla trekking – Lake Bunyonyi visit Have your breakfast and go for briefing about mountain gorilla trekking and later move to the forest for trekking, 1 hour on encounter with gorillas for taking pictures and observing their behaviors. After having lunch, Transfer to Lake Bunyonyi for relaxation. Dinner and overnight at Bunyonyi eco resort, kyahugye Island.

  • Day 9

    Transfer to Lake Mburo National Park Set off to Lake Mburo National Park after your breakfast, stopover at Igongo cultural center for lunch and proceed to Lake Mburo National Park. Dinner and overnight at Mihingo lodge.

  • Day 10

    Morning game drive and boat cruise Go for morning game drive and view many wild animals like zebras, giraffes, kobs, impalas, situatungas among others- lunch and go for boat cruise on Lake Mburo, enjoy more water loving animals like crocodiles, hippos, buffaloes among others. Dinner and overnight at Mihingo Safari lodge.

  • Day 11

    Transfer to Kampala. After breakfast, set off to Kampala, have an afternoon drive through the City and then drive to Entebbe. Dinner and overnight at 2Friends lodge/ Protea
