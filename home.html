COMPANY BACKGROUND

DANIZA Limited Trading Under Brand name Daniza Tours

DanizaDT was first operated as a sole proprietorship in South Sudan providing PMO transport services in 2007 and 2008, she later extended the provision of her services into Uganda operating under various projects undertaken by corporate entities and non-governmental organizations. In June 2013, Daniza limited was registered in South Sudan and September 2017 it was fully incorporated as a limited liability company in Uganda trading under the brand name Daniza tours. Since then, Daniza offers tailor-made tours, travel and vehicle rental packages to different clients with a wide fleet of vehicles for hire such as four-wheel land cruisers, safari vehicles, vans, buses, pickups, executive saloon cars, motorcycle and trucks.

Mission:
To complete our client's tours, travel and car rental needs in Uganda and in so doing,exceed their expectations for service, quality, and value.
Vision:

Objectives:
