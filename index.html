EXPLORE Daniza


Daniza was first operated as a sole proprietorship in South Sudan providing PMO transport services in 2007 and 2008, she later extended the provision of her services into Uganda operating under various projects undertaken by corporate entities and non-governmental organizations.

We have constant commitment to be the best socially and enviromentally responsible with 100% certified customer care. Uganda is beautiful and special, we want to share the pearl of Africa with you.
In June 2013, Daniza limited was registered in South Sudan and September 2017 it was fully incorporated as a limited liability company in Uganda trading under the brand name Daniza tours. Since then, Daniza offers tailor-made tours, travel and vehicle rental packages to different clients with a w ide fleet of vehicles.
