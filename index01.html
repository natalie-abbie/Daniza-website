Established in 2007 in Sudan, the following year of 2008, Daniza opened in Uganda and was incorporated as a limited liability company in 2017. A message from the the managing director clearly portrays the tourism enthusiast who loved travelling and guiding and thats where the travel business started. Daniza offers Uganda tour packages in safari parks, gorrilla tours, wildlife encounters, birding tours, adventure tours, cultural tours, car hire with a fleet of vehicles with a great reputation built on trust and professionalism
We have constant commitment to be the best socially and enviromentally responsible with 100% certified customer care. Uganda is beautiful and special, we want to share the pearl of Africa with you.
Our reservations team will help you design the perfect trip. we carefully select for you the comfort level accomodation to provide the perfect balance between price of quality offering private en suite facilities. Daniza offers unique tailor made tours, Group trips to Uganda and we also operate our own fleet of vehicles, with our own drivers and professional safari guides who ensure your safety and comfort
